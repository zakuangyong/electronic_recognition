Search: Recommended

Split the left column into three clear zones: primary search form, compact support tray, and persistent action/footer area.

Search Operations Panel

Recommended
1. Core Search Form Keyword, retrieval mode, filters, debug switch. Keep this visually dominant.
2. Compact Utility Tray Move health and demo queries into compact collapsible cards with tighter spacing.
3. Persistent Action Area Keep submit/rebuild controls visually anchored at the bottom of the form section.
Search Status Row
Use a light footer strip for online/degraded state and quick hints, instead of large blocks in the main stack.
